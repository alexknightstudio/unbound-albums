# Album market research — 2026-07-16

Deep-research pass over Zno, Artifact Uprising, Mixbook, Printique, Miller's
Lab, and Bay Photo (25 claims verified 3-vote adversarial, 0 refuted; all
against live vendor pages), plus a hands-on session in Alex's Printique
account. Drove the size-lineup/pricing/spread-count decisions of 2026-07-16.

## Page & spread model

- Lay-flat / flush-mount binding is universal for premium wedding albums.
  Full-bleed images across the center are an explicitly supported print spec.
- **Miller's Signature albums start AND end with full panorama spreads**
  (FAQ: "please design your first page as a panorama spread"). No single
  first/last pages in our fulfillment path — every unit is a full spread.
- Bay Photo offers two constructions: Seamless Page Spreads (creased at
  center, no cut) and Micro Gutter Spreads (1mm cut at spine). Either way the
  centerfold is physical: crossing it is fine, faces on it are not.
- Commercial model everywhere: base = 10 spreads (20 pages), pages added two
  at a time. Practical range on thick pages: 10–35 spreads.

## Print-file specs

- **Miller's (our spec): full-spread files at 250 DPI, NO added bleed,
  everything important 3/8"–1/2" from the file edge.** A 10×10 album spread
  is one 20"×10" file (5000×2500 px); 12×12 → 24"×12" (6000×3000 px).
- Printique (reference): 0.25" cover bleed, interior at actual size trimmed
  ~0.125" all sides.
- UNBOUND rule adopted: 0.5" safe zone from all trim edges; no faces or
  emphasis-photo centers of gravity on the fold.

## Sizes

- Market core, and the Miller's ∩ Bay Photo intersection: **10×10, 12×12,
  11×14**. Artifact Uprising: 7 sizes ($159–309 base). Zno ~9 unique dims.
  Miller's 13 (5×5–12×12, 15×10). Bay Pacific 8 (to 11×14/12×12).
- No sales data on the most popular size surfaced — 10×10 hero is Alex's
  professional judgment.

## Pricing (list, 2026-07-16)

| Vendor | 10×10-class base (10 spreads) | Per added spread |
|---|---|---|
| Artifact Uprising 10×10 | $219 | ~$11.50 |
| Artifact Uprising 12×12 | $289 | ~$13.50 |
| Mixbook 12×12 lay-flat | $144.99–179.99 | $7.38–9.98 |
| Printique Premium 10×10 (measured live) | $184.99 | $8.50 |

- Lay-flat carries an 80–90% premium over standard hardcover (Mixbook).
- Bay Photo Pacific pro list: ~$97 (small/10 sides) to ~$626 (12×12/70
  sides). Miller's includes prints in per-spread pricing; 1–2 day lab
  turnaround, free UPS next-day.
- Flat-price-with-real-page-count is a genuine differentiator — nobody does
  it — but only at honest numbers. Current $249/$279/$299 for 15 spreads is
  a placeholder pending DreamBooks Pro economics.

## Editor patterns observed at Printique (for Phase 4)

Live price in the order button; layout browser filtered by photo count;
Spread Layouts vs Page Layouts as distinct template classes; flip-spread
action; paper type/thickness as order-time upsells (+$60 heavy pages); foil
cover stamping $11.99/line; snapping + inch rulers + grid toggle;
Cover → End Pages → paired-page spreads filmstrip.
